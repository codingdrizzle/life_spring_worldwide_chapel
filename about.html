ABOUT US

LIFE SPRING WORLDWIDE CHAPEL, GHANA

This is a bible believing church which has been led by the spirit of God , to receive the Lord Jesus Christ as our lord and savior, and of the Son, and the Holy Spirit, we do now, in the presence of God Angels and this assembly, most solemnly and joyfully enter the covenant with one another as one body in Christ.


We engage therefore by the aid of the Holy Spirit to walk together in the christian love to strive for the advancement of this church in knowledge, holiness and comfort to promote its prosperity and spirituality to sustain its workshop, ordinance, discipline and doctrines to contribute cheerfully and regularly to support of the church and the expenses of the church the relief of the poor and the spread of the gospel through all nations.

AIMS AND OBJECTIVES
  • The purpose of this church shall be advancement of the kingdom of God.
  • To be a dynamic covenant and fellowship, of christians filled with the Holy spirit and practically loving one another.
  • To be worshipping fellowship, experience an awareness of Jesus Christ, recognizing his personas responding in obedience to his leadership.
  • To be Christ like in our daily living, standing at all times for the truth, holiness and in defense of the rights of the afflicted, the poor and the needy in this community and the world.
  • To help people experience a growing knowledge of God and his purpose for their lives in the world.
  • To be a church that ministers unselfishly to both the spiritual and physical needs of persons in this community by emphasizing total commitment of life personality and possession to the Lordship of Christ.
  • To witness for Christ in the power of the Holy spirit both in this community and throughout the world.
  • To be a ministry that co-operate with other Christian groups and churches in ministering to the world.
FAITH AND DOCTRINES
  1. We believe that the bible is written by men filled with the Holy Spirit.
  2. It receives the scripture as its authority in matters of faith and practice. Its understanding of Christian truth as contained therein is in essential accord with the relief of the church.
  3. We believe that man was created in holiness but by his own will, he fell into sin and out of harmony with God and is without hope in his sins
  4. We believe that the salvation of sinners is wholly of God's grace through the work of God's son who took upon himself our nature yet without sin and by his own obedience even in death paid for our sins. He is the risen Lord now living and pleading for us at God's throne. He is the only saviour for men.
  5. We believe that the great Gospel blessing which Christ secure to those who believe in complete fellowship with God. We are forgiven of sin, and made righteous before God by our faith in our redeemer's blood (blood of Christ), and brought into blessed peace and favour with God. We have every blessing needful for time and eternity.
  6. We believe that the blessings of salvation are made free to all by the Gospel . Nothing keeps any man from being saved except his own rejection of the Gospel. His rejection of Christ condemns him to eternal punishment.
  7. We believe that repentance and faith are our sacred duties. They are brought about by the power of the spirit of God to make us new. We know our sinful condition and turn to God, laying aside our sins and receive the Lord Jesus Christ as our saviour, priest and King. We lean upon him as the only and only all sufficient saviour.
  8. We believe that God is sovereign in the election and salvation of who believe in His son. He regenerates and saves winners and sanctifies believers.
  9. We believe that sanctification is the work of the Holy Spirit by which we are set apart from the world and are made partakers of his Holiness.
  10. We believe that the law of God is the eternal rule of moral government. This rule never changes and it is holy, just and good obedience to His holy law is possible only by power of God through Faith in Christ.
  11. We believe that a local church is a congregation of baptized believers, associated to a covenant or agreement in faith and fellowship of the Gospel of Jesus Christ working to carry on God's work and glorify His name.
  12. We believe that baptism is the immersion ( dipping) in water of a believer, in the name of the Father, Son, and Holy spirit. It is a picture of death to sin and rising to a new life in the dying love of Christ, only baptized believers can take part in it. However, church members and visiting Baptists / other believers are recommended to examine their lives before participation.
  13. We believe that the first day of the week is the lord's day. It is to be kept sacred for religious purpose and giving ourselves to worship, service and rest in His name.
  14. We believe that we should obey the civil government except when it opposes the will of our Lord Jesus Christ, as revealed in the scripture.
  15. We believe that there is great difference between the saved and the unsave. Only those who have shown faith in the Lord Jesus Christ are saved and made righteous. Those who remain away from Christ will remain lost in this life and in the life to come
  16. We believe that the end of this world is coming that in the last day Christ will descend from heaven and raise the dead from the grave that a separation will take place. The unbeliever will be given to everlasting punishment and righteous to endless joy. This judgement will fix forever the final state of man in heaven and hell on the principles of God's righteousness.
  17. We also believe curing of sickness with plants and herbs as it is in the scripture ( Ezekiel) and a revelation by God to the church through inspiration.
